About Me


About Helen Sinclair

Originated from Wellington, New Zealand; Helen is a mother of 2 children,works as a professional business woman and part time stay at home chef. She has migrated her family to Malaysia as she has found the environment there exciting and exotic. She is fond of tasting dishes from all over the globe including Asian cuisines such as Japanese, Chinese, Vietnamese and Malaysia. Her favourite including Malaysian dishes as they are easy and straight forward to prepare yet amazing in taste.

Helen enjoys writing journals, playing instruments and cooking for her children. She has a deep fondness of introducing the art of simple cooking to youths and wants to continue her dream by creating new ways to spread her knowledge.

The Recipes
