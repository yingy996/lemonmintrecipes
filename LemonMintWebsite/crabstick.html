Crispy Crab Sticks

15 June 2015

Ingredients

  • 500 Grams imitation crab sticks
  • 200ml cooking oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Gently unroll and peel the crab sticks, then separate roughly to 1 cm thin
  2. Arrange the crab sticks to one layer on plates and allow to air or dry for several hours until they are dried and slightly curled.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or wok. Test the temperature of the oil by dropping a piece of crab stick in it. The crab stick should sizzle, float upwards and flatten out instantly.
  4. Deep fry in small batches until lightly golden.
  5. Drain excess oil from crab sticks on paper towels and store in air-tight container
