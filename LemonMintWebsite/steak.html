Sirloin Steak With Garlic Butter

16 June 2015

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 pounds beef top sirloin steaks
  • Salt and pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat.
  2. In a small saucepan, melt butter over medium-low heat with garlic powder and minced garlic. Set aside.
  3. Sprinkle both sides of each steak with salt and pepper.
  4. Grill steaks 4 to 5 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. When done, transfer to warmed plates. Brushes tops liberally with garlic butter, and allow resting for 2 to 3 minutes before serving.
